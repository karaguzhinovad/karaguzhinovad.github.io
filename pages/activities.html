AIESEC

AIESEC is an international youth-run, non-governmental and not-for-profit organization that provides young people with leadership development, cross-cultural internships, and global volunteer exchange experiences. The organization focuses on empowering young people to make a progressive social impact.

MUN

Model United Nations is an academic simulation of the United Nations where students play the role of delegates from different countries and attempt to solve real world issues with the policies and perspectives of their assigned country. For example, a student may be assigned the United Kingdom and will have to solve global topics such as nuclear non-proliferation or climate change from the policies and perspectives of the United Kingdom.

Voluntering

Volunteering is all about you being able to contribute some of your own time to help people in our communities. Volunteers support our communities and add to them in their own unique way using a wide variety of skills, knowledge and experience.

I was delighted to be part of the MUN community, primarily because of how conferences were planned. Some time later, it was decided to organize the same event at the school where I study. Watching the experience of my schoolmates and me through social networks, a large number of people from our city began to show interest because this type of activity had never been conducted in our city before. That is why we invited students from all over the region. I have been in charge of organizing the regional Model United Nations Conference in which I was also a chair of the committee “Economic and Social Council”.
